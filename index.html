Who are we?

Greg McKelvey is a web developer for Twenty Ideas, a local Eugene tech company. Greg used FreeCodeCamp.org to change careers from teaching high school to coding. Still retaining his passion for teaching, he would be more than welcome to show you some of his favorite tools and resources that he used to bootstrap his new career in tech! (Gatsby anyone?!)

Nikole Gipps is a WordPress Engineer for X-Team, an Australia-based developer-centric company with remote staff all over the world. She loves everything server-side, but will occasionally make her way frontward. Nikole brings over two decades of professional web development experience to our code camp metups — but she'd say she's always still learning.

10/22 Women in Tech Week: Not Your Average Tech Jobs w/ Remote Workforce Panel

**THIS IS A TICKETED EVENT**

The tech industry is home to a wide variety of career options, many of which are not primarily responsible for writing or analyzing code. Come learn about roles with less traditional educational paths like Product Owners, Business Analysts, Quality Assurance, and more. These career pathways often create a bridge for those interested in transitioning between industries.

Join us for a live Q&A facilitated by Elise Lockwood (Qualitative Research Director, DORIS Research) as she interviews experienced professionals who not only work in these careers, they do it remotely. Engage with local experts, discover what these professionals deal with on a daily basis as well as what they face in the quest toward career development.

Several local organizations that support career preparation will be on site and available to answer questions about how their programs may benefit you.

10/25 Women in Tech Week: Ask Me Anything with Tech Professionals

Bring a lunch and hang out with several members of the tech community for a candid Ask Me Anything session. Want to know about working in tech, getting started, what we're excited about now, or anything else? Join us!

Participants include: Nikole Gipps, WordPress Engineer for X-Team (back end web / full stack LAMP); Megan Landers, Director, Technical Account Manager at Tanium (security); Erin Zygaitis, DevOps Apprentice at City of Eugene (servers); Nate Nichols, Front End Developer at Concentric Sky (front end web / functional programming); and Seth Dudenhofer, Application Analyst II at McKenzie-Willamette Medical Center (Python).

All genders are welcome but please remember that this is an inclusive and welcoming space for all!
